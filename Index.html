05 — How This Works

The Terms. Clear and Simple.

Minimum Commitment

All tiers run for a minimum of 3 months. This is a long term partnership, not a one-off. Short commitments do not build real cultural presence.

Payment

50% upfront before any content is produced. Remaining 50% at the start of month two. This is standard practice for all Sababa partnerships.

Product

Verborgen provides product for all features. Product is part of the partnership at every tier and is not included in the monthly fee.

Creative Control

All content is created within Sababa's creative direction and aesthetic. Verborgen will be consulted but Sababa retains creative ownership of execution. This is what makes the content valuable.

Content Rights

Content produced lives on Sababa's platform. Verborgen may reshare all content. Exclusive co-created content is jointly owned by both brands.

Renewal

At the end of the minimum term both parties review and decide on continuation. Rates are locked for the agreed term and renegotiated at renewal.
